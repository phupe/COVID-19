Coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic time-series of the number of deaths accross the most affected countries plus Japan and South Korea.
Forecast of the evolution of the time-series. Two prediction models are available (choose the model to display with the button on the graphs):
  • Predict (L): simple a naive growth curve prediction model based on a logictic function. This model tends to underestimate the predicted values.
  • Predict (R): simple a naive growth curve prediction model based on a Richards' curve. This model seems to better fit the data but the predicted values may be less reliable when few data are available.
